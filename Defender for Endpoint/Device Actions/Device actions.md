When investigating a device you can perform actions, collect data, or remotely access the machine.<br>
You can perform:<br>
*Containment*<br>
* Isolate Device
* Restrict app execution
* Run antivirus

*Investigation*<br>
* Initiate Automated Investigation
* Collect investigation package
* Initiate Live Response Session<br><br>
  *Antivirus Scan on devices:As a part of investigation or response process, you can remotely initiate an antivirus scan.Can also Initiate automated Investigations*
